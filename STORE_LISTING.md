## Description* (paste into the big text box)

LoopMail lets you preview audio loops directly in Gmail—no need to open each email.

Built for music producers who receive loops via email. A playback bar appears below the Gmail search bar. It plays the first 10 seconds of each audio attachment (MP3, WAV, FLAC, AIFF, OGG, WebM), then auto-advances to the next. Browse your inbox while your loops play in the background.

Features:
• 10-second previews — hear each loop, then move on
• Search sync — queue matches your Gmail search (e.g. "drake", "rnb")
• Shuffle — randomize the order
• One-click download — save loops you like
• Keyboard shortcuts — Alt+Left/Right to skip, Alt+Space to pause

Free for 1 week, then $4.99/month.
