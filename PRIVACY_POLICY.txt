LoopMail Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

What LoopMail Does
LoopMail is a Chrome extension that lets you preview audio attachments from your Gmail inbox. A playback bar appears in Gmail and plays 10-second snippets of audio files (MP3, WAV, FLAC, etc.) from your emails.

Data We Collect
• Email address — Used to verify your subscription and link your account.
• Gmail access — We request read-only access to your Gmail inbox to fetch email metadata and audio attachments for playback. We only read emails that match your current view/search.
• Payment info — If you subscribe, payment is handled by Stripe. We do not store your card details.

How We Use Your Data
• To provide the extension (play audio from your inbox).
• To verify your subscription status.
• To process payments (via Stripe).

Data Storage
• OAuth tokens are stored locally in your browser.
• We do not store your emails or audio files on our servers. Audio is streamed from Gmail's API to your browser for playback.

Data Sharing
We do not sell your data. We share data only with:
• Google (OAuth, Gmail API) — for sign-in and inbox access
• Stripe — for payment processing

Your Rights
You can disconnect your Gmail account or cancel your subscription at any time from the extension settings. You can also uninstall the extension to remove all local data.
